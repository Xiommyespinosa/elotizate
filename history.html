    • HISTORY

    It was a cloudy day, when 3 students from the Technological Baccalaureate of El Grullo from room QB2, thought about what they could do for their sixth semester integrative project, they had several options, all of them food since they considered that it was what people always look to buy.

    They had as options to sell waffles or corn, Naomi, one of the team members, Naomi, is an expert in corn, she has many skills to prepare and sell them, so they decided to choose that option as an integrating project, and create new combinations of corn , Naomi would teach Diana and Tania, the other members of the team, to prepare the corn to be able to sell it.

    During the first sale, they would sell glasses with corn, tostielotes and maruchan with corn, the three were very excited and nervous since none of the 3 had made a maruchan soup before. At the beginning of the sale, during the first break, they began to heat the water since they already had orders for maruchans with corn, but something they never took into account was the number of customers they would have, which was quite a lot, so they began to get stressed by seeing so many customers and being a bit inexperienced, the business was a bit disorganized and when preparing the maruchans, Diana spilled the hot water on the extension of the light and it exploded, the three were very scared but they had to quickly get a new extension and continue working, so they did and they were selling very well until Tania and Naomi, at a time of a lot of work, crossed paths and collided, but this was not the only bad thing, if not until in that clash the pot of corn got in their way and fell, luckily for the company not all the corn fell but only a third of it, which was bad but not as bad as if the whole pot had fallen.

    This remained as a lesson for them so that in future sales they would be better organized and that is how it was, during their foreign sales they were better organized, they moved the electric extension cord away from the pot of corn and the water from the maruchan. They also learned and became more skilled at preparing maruchan soups. One afternoon Naomi assigned it to train Diana and Tania, who were the most inexperienced in the company, and now the three vendors are skilled at preparing, selling and charging for the products.

    All three learned that despite every mistake and frustration along the sales journey, there are always learnings and new skills.
